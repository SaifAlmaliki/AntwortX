How it works

Four steps from brand setup to publishing. Your team can still repeat or reorder steps as needed.

  1. 1

    Define your brand

    Create a brand profile from your site URL or edit it by hand—voice, visuals, and positioning stay in one record everyone can reuse.

  2. 2

    Create on-brand assets

    Generate influencers, ads, thumbnails, and newsletters that pull from the same profile so creative does not drift.

  3. 3

    Find prospects and email them

    Build targeted lists, then send a short sequence of personalized cold emails and track replies and opt-outs.

  4. 4

    Publish and review results

    Schedule posts to social, then use engagement and analytics in the app to see what resonates and where to focus next.
